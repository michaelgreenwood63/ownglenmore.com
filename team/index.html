About the Team

Mark Jontz & Associates built OwnGlenmore.com as a resource for buyers, sellers, and investors who want expert, street-level knowledge of Kelowna's largest and most active neighbourhood.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling a property you've held for years, or looking for Kelowna's best investment value — we know every street, every price point, and every micro-market within Old Glenmore and North Glenmore. Nobody knows this neighbourhood like we do.

MJ
Team Lead
Mark Jontz
REALTOR® · Team Lead

Since his start in 1999, Mark Jontz has been one of the top-producing agents in the entire BC Okanagan. Mark was inducted into the Chairman's Club Lifetime Membership in 2014, after receiving the award for 10 straight years starting in 2004. His 2024 induction into the Chairman's Hall of Fame — the first in brokerage history — has solidified him as the go-to Okanagan expert.

With Kelowna being home since 1967, Mark has the answers to any questions you may have about the area — with 5,000+ home evaluations and 3,000+ transactions under his belt, there is no sale too complicated. Mark has seen success through down markets, up markets, and flat markets. Whether the property is large or small, Mark treats every client with the same white glove service. If you want it sold quickly, for top dollar, with the least amount of inconvenience — call Mark Jontz & Associates.
