The Neighbourhood

Glenmore, Kelowna

Originally "Dry Valley" — too arid for farming when the rest of the Okanagan was already settled — Glenmore was transformed after the Glenmore Irrigation District planted 100,000 fruit trees in 1912. Today it's home to 24,244 residents across two MLS sub-areas, stretching from established character homes and the Okanagan Golf Club near downtown all the way to master-planned communities, active orchards, and new development in the north. It's Kelowna's most diverse neighbourhood by housing type, lifestyle, and price point.

Sub-Area
Glenmore

The established heart of the valley — character homes on generous lots, heritage properties, the Okanagan Golf Club (36 holes), Kelowna Golf & Country Club, Glenmore Landing shopping, and Knox Mountain Park access. Minutes from downtown Kelowna.

Why Glenmore

The case for buying in Glenmore

🌳
Orchard Roots, Modern Growth

Glenmore's agricultural history gives it a character no other Kelowna neighbourhood has. Active orchards and farmlands still dot North Glenmore alongside master-planned communities — a rare blend of Okanagan heritage and new development.

⛳
Okanagan Golf Club

A 36-hole facility in your backyard — the Nicklaus-designed Bear Course and the Les Furber Quail Course. One of the premier golf destinations in Western Canada, minutes from your front door.

🎓
UBCO at Your Doorstep

UBC Okanagan is 10 minutes away via John Hindle Drive (opened 2018). Strong rental demand from students and faculty, plus proximity to Kelowna International Airport makes North Glenmore a natural investment corridor.

🏔️
Trails From Every Angle

Knox Mountain Park, Dilworth Mountain Park, Stephens Coyote Ridge (102 hectares), Glenmore Ridge Trail, Brandt's Creek Linear Park, Blair Pond Park — and the Okanagan Rail Trail running all the way to Lake Country and Vernon.

🏗️
Kelowna's Biggest Growth Story

The North Glenmore Sector Study (2026) identifies 4,000 acres for future growth — the population could double to 35,000. Wilden's Phase 3 (1,000 homes), Burtch Road Extension, and the $251M Parkinson Recreation Centre rebuild are all underway.

🏫
Schools Built for the Future

Five existing schools including French Immersion and Montessori options. A $101M Burtch Road Middle School (800 seats) is approved and funded. A 1,500-seat Glenmore Secondary School is in the OCP. The educational infrastructure is being built ahead of the growth.

Education

Schools in Glenmore

Glenmore is served by School District 23 with five schools currently operating and three major new schools approved or in planning — built ahead of the growth.

Elementary · K–6
North Glenmore Elementary

Community-focused school known for its arts and nature programs. Serves the North Glenmore corridor.

Elementary · K–6
Watson Road Elementary

Inclusive school recognized for its academics and supportive environment. Central Glenmore location.

Elementary · K–6 · French Immersion
École Glenmore Elementary

Offers a full French Immersion curriculum — one of the few immersion options on this side of Kelowna.

Private · All Ages
Coronation Montessori

Private Montessori school emphasizing individualized learning. One of Kelowna's most established alternative education options.

Middle School · Gr. 7–9
École Dr. Knox Middle School

Full range of academic programs and extracurricular activities. Feeds from all three Glenmore elementaries.

Coming Soon

Burtch Road Middle School — $101M approved project adding 800 student seats. Glenmore Secondary School — 1,500-seat campus-style school in the OCP, integrated with the Parkinson Recreation Centre expansion. New Wilden Elementary — proposed to serve the growing Wilden community.

New Construction

New developments in Glenmore

Glenmore has more active development than any other Kelowna neighbourhood. Here's what's selling and what's coming.

🏘️
Wilden — Phase 3

BC's largest master-planned community. 1,800 homes completed of a planned 2,800. Phase 3 envisions 1,000 more homes across 578 acres (227 protected). Development projected to begin ~2030. OCP and zoning amendments in progress now.

🏠
Antler Heights at Wilden

Now selling — semi-detached walkout rancher duplexes ranging from 1,953 to 2,791 sq ft. Pricing from $1,229,900 + GST. Curated collections within the Wilden community.

🏙️
GlenPark Row I & II

52 + 77 modern townhomes in North Glenmore. Three-level homes with 3–4 bedrooms, den, double garages, and private patios. 1,440–1,715 sq ft. GlenPark Row II from the high $600,000s.

🏗️
Trellis — 720 Valley Road

49 contemporary townhomes with 3–4 bedrooms, up to 2,363 sq ft. Currently selling from approximately $769,900 to $959,900. One of the most competitively priced new townhome projects in Glenmore.

🏢
Wilden Market Square

Proposed 6-storey mid-rise at 1521 Union Road featuring condos and townhomes. Plans include daycare, health services, and ground-floor retail — effectively a new town centre for the Wilden community.

🌟
Highpointe

Luxury gated community offering multi-million dollar estates with panoramic views overlooking the Glenmore Valley. For buyers looking at the top end of the Kelowna market in a private, established setting.
