Old Glenmore

Kelowna's most connected sub-area — centred on Highway 33, close to every major amenity, and 45 minutes from Big White.

About Old Glenmore

The north end of Glenmore

Old Glenmore sits north of Highway 33 — the boundary that divides Glenmore's two sub-areas. It's close to the commercial corridor, Ben Lee Park, and all major transit routes. A mix of established single-family homes, newer builds, and purpose-built condos makes it one of Kelowna's most varied markets.

🏞️
Ben Lee Park

A full recreation destination in Old Glenmore — skate park, water park, sports fields, BMX track, and a summer concession. One of Kelowna's best community parks.

🛒
Everything Nearby

Save-On-Foods, YMCA, walk-in clinics, dentists, and restaurants right along Highway 33 — plus Costco and Walmart a 10-minute drive away.

🏘️
Varied Housing Stock

From entry-level condos and mobile homes to newer single-family builds — Old Glenmore has more property type variety than almost anywhere else in Kelowna.
