Glenmore

The established heart of the valley — character homes, world-class golf, Knox Mountain trails, and minutes from downtown Kelowna.

About Glenmore

Kelowna's original orchard valley

Glenmore is where the neighbourhood began — established character homes on generous lots, mature trees, and a lifestyle that blends urban convenience with outdoor access. The Okanagan Golf Club (36 holes) and Kelowna Golf & Country Club sit within the sub-area. Knox Mountain Park, Kelowna's largest at 502 hectares, is minutes away. Glenmore Landing and surrounding commercial strips handle day-to-day errands. Downtown Kelowna is a 10–15 minute drive.

⛳
World-Class Golf

Two 18-hole courses in your backyard — the Okanagan Golf Club's Nicklaus-designed Bear Course and the Les Furber Quail Course. Kelowna Golf & Country Club (est. 1920) rounds out one of BC's best golf corridors.

🏔️
Knox Mountain Access

Knox Mountain Park — 502 hectares of trails, lookout points, and natural landscape — is one of Kelowna's most-used outdoor destinations and is easily accessible from Glenmore's established streets.

🏘️
Character Homes & Mature Streets

Glenmore has the established character homes, larger lots, and mature landscaping that newer subdivisions simply can't replicate. Heritage properties including the Ireland Farm House (1919) reflect the valley's orchard history.
