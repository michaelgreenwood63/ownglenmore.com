Glenmore · Neighbourhood Guide

Amenities & Lifestyle

Glenmore has more amenities per square kilometre than almost anywhere else in Kelowna — groceries, parks, recreation, faith communities, and everything in between, all without leaving the neighbourhood.

🛒
Grocery & Daily Needs
Everything on one run

Glenmore's grocery options cover every budget and cuisine preference. Major chains, budget stores, and a strong selection of ethnic grocers reflecting the neighbourhood's diverse community.

Save-On-Foods
Full Service Grocery

Premium grocery with an in-store pharmacy, deli, bakery, and floral department. One of the best-stocked stores in the Glenmore corridor.

South Asian & Ethnic Grocers
Specialty Grocery

Glenmore's large South Asian and Filipino communities are served by several specialty grocers stocking imported staples, spices, and fresh produce you won't find at the chains.

Shoppers Drug Mart
Pharmacy + Convenience

Full pharmacy, convenience grocery, beauty, and health products. Convenient for top-ups between bigger shops.

Pharmasave
Pharmacy

Local independent pharmacy with personalized service. Good alternative to chain pharmacies for prescriptions and OTC medications.

🛍️
Shopping & Retail
Highway 33 has it covered

The Highway 33 corridor is lined with retail, services, and strip malls — from big-box stores to local shops. Most errands can be done without leaving the neighbourhood.

Highway 33 Corridor
Main Retail Strip

The main commercial artery of Old Glenmore — lined with restaurants, service businesses, banks, and retail from one end to the other. Costco and Walmart are a 10-minute drive away.

Home Hardware
Hardware & Home

Full hardware, tools, paint, and home improvement supplies. Locally owned and well-stocked — no drive across town needed for your weekend project.

Dollarama
Discount Retail

Household goods, cleaning products, party supplies, and seasonal items at dollar-store prices. Practical and convenient for everyday needs.

Banks & Credit Unions
Financial Services

Multiple major banks and credit unions on the Hwy 33 strip — TD, RBC, Interior Savings, and others within minutes of any Glenmore address.

Restaurants & Fast Food
Dining

A wide range of dining options along Hwy 33 — from national chains (McDonald's, Tim Hortons, A&W) to local Indian, Filipino, and Canadian restaurants.

Orchard Park Mall
Shopping Mall · Nearby

Kelowna's main shopping mall is 10 minutes from Glenmore — anchored by the Bay and Sporting Life, with 150+ stores, a food court, and a cinema.

🌿
Parks & Trails
Greenspace at your door

North Glenmore, in particular, is defined by its proximity to Kelowna's best urban greenspace. You don't need to drive to find a trail or a park — it's already in the neighbourhood.

Mission Creek Greenway
Trail System · North Glenmore

One of Kelowna's most beloved trail systems follows Mission Creek along Glenmore's western edge. Paved and unpaved paths for walking, running, and cycling — extending all the way to Lakeshore Road and Lake Okanagan.

Ben Lee Park
Recreation Park · North Glenmore

Glenmore's flagship park — a full recreation destination with a skate park, outdoor water park, BMX track, sports fields, and a summer concession stand. Families spend entire days here.

Glenmore Lions Park
Neighbourhood Park

A well-maintained community park with playgrounds, picnic areas, and open green space. Popular with young families in the surrounding streets.

Bellevue Creek Greenway
Trail · Natural Area

A quieter natural trail system connecting through south Kelowna — wooded, natural, and popular with dog walkers and trail runners.

Gallagher's Canyon
Golf & Scenery · Nearby

One of BC's most scenic golf courses sits just southeast of Glenmore — with canyon views and Okanagan landscape that's hard to match. A short drive from any Glenmore address.

Neighbourhood Pocket Parks
Local Parks

Glenmore is scattered with smaller neighbourhood parks — playgrounds, benches, and green strips tucked into residential streets throughout both North and South sub-areas.

🏒
Recreation & Sports
Stay active, stay close

From the YMCA to community arenas, Glenmore has serious recreational infrastructure — and Big White Ski Resort is 45 minutes straight up Highway 33.

YMCA Kelowna
Fitness & Recreation

Full-service YMCA on the Hwy 33 corridor — gym, fitness classes, swimming pool, and youth programs. One of the best community fitness facilities in the Okanagan.

Glenmore Arena
Arena & Events

Glenmore's own community arena — home to minor hockey, public skating, and local events. Right in the neighbourhood, no drive required.

Glenmore Centennial Hall
Community Hall

Glenmore's own community hall hosts local events, meetings, youth programs, and community gatherings year-round.

Ben Lee Park Skate Park
Skateboarding · Free

A proper skate park inside Ben Lee Park — concrete bowls and street features that draw skaters from across Kelowna. Open and free year-round.

Ben Lee Park Water Park
Outdoor Water Play · Seasonal

A free outdoor spray and water play park operating through the summer months. Hands-down one of the most popular spots for families with young kids in all of Kelowna.

Big White Ski Resort
Skiing & Snowboarding · 45 min

Highway 33 runs straight to Big White — Kelowna's home ski mountain and one of BC's top resorts. 45 minutes from Glenmore, no connecting highway required.

⛪
Faith & Community
A neighbourhood of neighbours

Glenmore is one of Kelowna's most culturally diverse neighbourhoods — home to a wide range of faith communities, cultural organizations, and community gathering spaces.

Gurdwara Sahib Kelowna
Sikh Temple

Serving Glenmore's large and longstanding Punjabi-Sikh community — one of the largest Gurdwaras in the Okanagan, with regular services, langar (community kitchen), and cultural programs.

Glenmore United Church
Christian · United

An established congregation in the heart of Glenmore — welcoming, community-focused, and active in local outreach and programming.

St. Theresa Catholic Parish
Christian · Catholic

Catholic parish serving the Glenmore community with regular masses, sacraments, youth groups, and faith formation programs.

Evangelical & Baptist Churches
Christian · Various

Several evangelical and Baptist congregations operate in Glenmore, including Grace Baptist and others — offering a range of worship styles and community programming.

Muslim Community (Masjid)
Islamic

Glenmore's growing Muslim community is served by mosque facilities in the broader Kelowna area — reflecting the neighbourhood's continued cultural growth and diversity.

Cultural & Community Societies
Cultural Organizations

Various cultural associations — Filipino, South Asian, and others — hold events, festivals, and community gatherings in and around Glenmore throughout the year.

🏥
Health & Medical
Clinics, dentists, and specialists

Glenmore's Highway 33 corridor is lined with medical and dental offices, walk-in clinics, and pharmacies. Kelowna General Hospital is 10 minutes away.

Walk-In Clinics
Primary Care · No Appointment

Multiple walk-in medical clinics operate along the Hwy 33 strip — accessible without an appointment for non-emergency care, prescriptions, and referrals.

Kelowna General Hospital
Hospital · ~10 min

Kelowna's main hospital is approximately 10 minutes from Glenmore — a full-service hospital with emergency, surgical, and specialist services.

Dental Offices
Dentistry

Several dental clinics operate within Glenmore, offering general dentistry, orthodontics, and hygiene services — most with flexible appointment hours.

Physiotherapy & Chiro
Allied Health

Physiotherapy, chiropractic, and massage therapy clinics are scattered throughout Glenmore — convenient for athletes, tradespeople, and anyone managing chronic pain or injury recovery.

Optometrists
Eye Care

Eye clinics and optometrists on the Hwy 33 corridor offering exams, prescriptions, and glasses — including options with walk-in availability for urgent concerns.

Veterinary Clinics
Pet Care

Glenmore has veterinary clinics serving the neighbourhood's many pet-owning families — routine care, vaccinations, and emergency referrals available locally.

🚌
Getting Around
Well connected in every direction

Glenmore sits at Kelowna's geographic centre — well-served by transit, close to the highway network, and within 15 minutes of just about everything in the city.

BC Transit — Hwy 33 Routes
Public Transit

Multiple BC Transit routes run the length of Highway 33 connecting Glenmore to downtown Kelowna, Orchard Park, UBCO, and other key destinations. Frequent service during peak hours.

Highway 33
Main Arterial Road

The spine of Glenmore — connecting north to Hwy 97 and the rest of Kelowna, and southeast directly to Big White Ski Resort (45 min). Fast access to everything.

Kelowna International Airport (YLW)
Airport · ~10–15 min

YLW is one of Canada's fastest-growing airports and is a short drive from Glenmore — direct flights to Vancouver, Calgary, Toronto, Seattle, and seasonal routes.

UBCO Campus
University · ~10 min

UBC Okanagan's main campus is 10–15 minutes north of Glenmore via Hwy 97. A strong driver of rental demand for investors in the Glenmore market.

Downtown Kelowna
City Centre · ~15 min

Glenmore is roughly 15 minutes from downtown Kelowna by car — the waterfront, Prospera Place, restaurants, and the city's main business district are all within easy reach.

Big White Ski Resort
Mountain Access · 45 min

Glenmore's biggest geographic advantage — Highway 33 runs straight up the mountain. No ferry, no connecting highway, no detours. Just 45 minutes from your driveway to the chairlift.
