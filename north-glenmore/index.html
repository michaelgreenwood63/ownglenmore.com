North Glenmore

Glenmore's quieter, more residential end — families, retirees, and buyers who want greenspace at their doorstep.

About North Glenmore

The south end of Glenmore

North Glenmore sits south of Highway 33 — the quieter, more purely residential half of Glenmore. It borders Mission Creek Greenway along its western edge, giving residents direct access to one of Kelowna's best trail systems. The housing stock leans toward established single-family homes, ranchers, and older character properties — with prices that still represent some of the best value in the city.

🌿
Mission Creek Greenway

One of Kelowna's most beloved trail systems runs along North Glenmore's western edge — walking and cycling trails connecting the neighbourhood all the way to Lakeshore Road and Lake Okanagan.

🏡
Established Neighbourhoods

North Glenmore has a settled, residential feel — mature trees, larger lots, and homes built with families in mind. Away from the Highway 33 corridor and its traffic.

💰
Best Value Detached

Some of Kelowna's best value in single-family housing. North Glenmore delivers more square footage and lot size per dollar than most comparable Kelowna neighbourhoods.
